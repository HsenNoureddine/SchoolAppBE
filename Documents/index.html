Arabic Specialists
Arabic is at the heart of our work at RadixLingua. We are totally cognizant of the value of this language. When using Arabic, we strive to ensure accuracy, avoiding what is weak, which has become so common that only experts can discriminate the accurate and pure from the erroneous and flawed.

We translate from and into Arabic. When working from Arabic, we endeavor to produce a target text that reflects the gist of the original, faithfully indicating the intent of the author, but which holds, at the same time, the spirit of the language to which it is translated, abiding by its laws and respecting its rules.
When we translate into Arabic, our primary concern is to ensure that the Arabic translation is fathomable, and that it keeps up with the new terms, meanings and language usages that this language came to enclose - which have been thrust by exposure to others, but which do not blemish its principles or disrupt its morphology.

The challenge we are taking on is considerable, as we seek to adhere to what is right and avoid imprecision. However, it is worthwhile all efforts and weariness, as the recompense is greater and more evident.

Our willingness to seek precision prompts us to endeavor to produce a translation free of flaws in every language into which we translate. However, our focus on Arabic is excused because we are natives of this language and as the Arabic adage goes “the people of Mecca are more familiar with its alleys”.
Nonetheless, we seek to produce the best in every language listed. We thus put ourselves in the readers’ shoes, reading all what we produce with the eye of the objective critique.
Industries & Languages
In addition to texts of general content, our language services extend to encompass the following industries: the socio-political, the legal, the economic, the religious, the technical and the scientific, and we provide our language services for those fields in both directions between Arabic, English, French and Farsi, but also from Farsi into English.
About Us
Radix Lingua is a comprehensive Language Services Agency committed to excellence. Our methodology has two pillars: Quality of Service guaranteed by a team of reliable experts, and a pliable approach which allows us to model our services around each and every client. our mission is to offer a specialized translation, by acting as the intermediary between our clients and their readers. We ultimately seek to bridge the gap between what is communicated and what is understood. we are based in Beirut, and we provide our services to local businesses, regional customers, international organisations, research centers and think tanks.

Services

Editing
Re-read a text and highlight syntactic mistakes, but also grammatical and typographical ones, and make edits accordingly.
ProofReading
Re-read a text addressing only grammatical and typographical mistakes and make changes accordingly.
Transcription
Convert the content of any type of audio documents - in any of the aforementioned languages- into written material.
Summarizing
Capture the most important content of any written document, and reproduce an abridged, accurate, and coherent version of it.
Translation
Translate books, texts, articles, theses, papers, and speeches of any denomination .
Sworn Translation
Translate any document requiring the stamp of a sworn translator, from and into Arabic, English and French.
Reviewing
Compare a translation to the original text, highlight any discrepancies in the Translated Text, and make edits accordingly.
